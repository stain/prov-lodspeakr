prov:involved op back to qualified properties

IRI:http://www.w3.org/ns/prov#involved

Subproperties of prov:involved may be be qualified by creating instances of a corresponding prov:Involvement class. For example, the binary relation :baking prov:used :spoon can be qualified by asserting :baking prov:qualified [ a prov:Usage; prov:entity :baking; :foo :bar ] prov:involved should not be used without also using the subproperty. Subproperties of prov:involved may also be asserted directly without being qualified.
